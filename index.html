About Us

Headstone of the Corner Foundation is a non-profit committed to practical, community-led solutions that restore hope to vulnerable people across Nigeria.

Our Vision

A Nigeria where every person has the essentials to thrive — food, learning, health, and opportunity.

Our Mission

To mobilize resources and volunteers to deliver relief, expand access to education and healthcare, and equip youths and caregivers with life-changing skills.

Our Programs

Food Relief

Monthly community outreaches that deliver food baskets and essentials to low-income households.

  • Community mapping to reach the most in need
  • Nutrition-focused packages for children & elderly

Education Support

Scholarships, school kits, and after-school clubs that keep children learning and dreaming.

  • Back-to-school drives & book banks
  • Mentoring & digital skills clubs

Health & Skills Outreach

Pop-up clinics, health talks, and short vocational trainings for youths and caregivers.

  • Check-ups & health education
  • Tailoring, graphic design, and micro-enterprise basics
